# 📊 Regional Sales Analysis Using MySQL & Power BI
## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates end-to-end sales analytics using MySQL for querying and Power BI for visualization. The dataset covers regional sales performance across customers, products, and geographies, enabling business intelligence reporting and data-driven decision-making.

### Key goals of this project:

Analyze sales revenue, profit, and growth trends.

Identify top-performing products, customers, and regions.

Compare budget vs actual sales performance.

Perform customer segmentation and detect repeat purchase behavior.

Optimize warehouse & logistics analysis for operational efficiency.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

SQL (MySQL) – Data cleaning, wrangling, and advanced analysis queries

Power BI – KPI dashboards & interactive reports

Excel – Preprocessing and data formatting

## 🔍 Analysis Areas
### 1. Sales Performance

Total revenue & profit per year

Monthly sales trends & peak months

YoY growth in revenue and profit

Revenue by sales channel (Wholesale, Export, Distributor)

### 2. Product Insights

Top 5 products by revenue & quantity

Profitability analysis (unit cost vs unit price)

Budget vs actual revenue comparison (2017)

Least profitable but frequently sold products

Frequently bought product combinations (market basket analysis)

### 3. Regional Performance

Revenue by state/region

High-income states with low sales performance

Revenue per capita by geography

Regional sales comparisons (South, West, etc.)

### 4. Customer Behavior

Top 5 customers by total revenue

Unique customers per year

Repeat purchase analysis

Customers with highest YoY spending growth

Average revenue & profit by customer segment

### 5. Operations & Logistics

Warehouse-level order and revenue analysis

Average delivery size (quantity & revenue)

Average revenue per order by warehouse
